Class project for CS 545 at UW-Madison. Provides an open-source Spanish conjugation driller. Presents user with a sentence that has an input field in place of a verb in the sentence. Information on the missing verb, including infinitive and relevant conjugation information, is shown under the input field. The user is asked to fill in the correct conjugation and then press *Check Answer*. The user will then receive feedback on their answer and if they want to they can choose *Get New Exercise* to get a new random sentence from the corpus.
